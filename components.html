Cards

Base Card

Base Cards have no styling: you can add class modifiers to style them.

Base Card 
      <div class="card">
        Basic Card
      </div>

Full Card

Fill the background of a card with .card-full

Full Card 
      <div class="card card-full">
        Full Card
      </div>

Coloured Cards

Use .card-bg-blue or .card-bg-orange to colour the card backgrounds

Blue Card
Orange Card 
      <div class="card card-bg-blue">
        Blue Card
      </div>
      <div class="card card-bg-orange">
        Orange Card
      </div>

Bordered Cards

Use .card-bordered-blue or .card-bordered-orange to border the cards. This works with .card-full

Blue Bordered Card
Orange Bordered Card 
      <div class="card card-bordered-blue">
        Blue Bordered Card
      </div>
      <div class="card card-bordered-orange">
        Orange Bordered Card
      </div>

Text Utilities

Text Colour

Use .text-blue and .text-orange to colourize your text!

This normal text turns into blue text.
This normal text turns into blue orange. 

      This normal text turns into <span class="text-blue">blue text</span>.
      This normal text turns into <span class="text-orange">orange text</span>.

Text Alignment

Use .text-* to align your text.

This text is left-aligned.
This text is centered-aligned.
This text is right-aligned.

      <div class="text-left">This text is left-aligned.</div>
      <div class="text-center">This text is centered-aligned.</div>
      <div class="text-right">This text is right-aligned.</div>

Shape Utilities

Rounded Corners

Use .rounded to round elements.

Rounded Orange Card
Rounded Blue Bordered Card 
      <div class="card card-bg-orange rounded">
        Orange Card
      </div>
      <div class="card card-bordered-blue rounded">
        Blue Bordered Card
      </div>

Responsive Blocks

Use .block-responsive to make block-level elements like img responsive (try it out by resizing your browser)
